Training

Engage

Duration: 3 days

Engage is a three day workshop that will introduce you to a set of pragmatic tools and methods, that you can use to 'tune' your IT Department.

With a thorough discussion of automated builds, deployments, and versioning we will overcome a majority of the drudgery in application development. (builds, deployments, analysis, databases, assemblyline, versioning, warmup)

An overview of architectures that are available to us with today's technologies will enable a more predictable work flow and one that is easy to test against. (viking, sharding, insert only, memcache, multi-tenant?, message based, IoComm)

Outcome / What to Expect?

This course will push you to expand your mind and challenge the conventional wisdom.

Topics

  • Automated Builds
  • Automated Deployments
  • Inversion of Control
  • Inversion of Communication
  • Intermediate NHibernate Concepts
